About Us


WE ARE CODEGYM - WORKING TO BRING MORE VALUE TO LIFE

Information Technology is a large and important industry of Vietnam, but the shortage of human resources in both quantity and quality is always a barrier to development.

Human resources are the most important key to promoting the development of the country's IT industry. Understanding that situation, the founders of CodeGym - who were originally experienced programmers, lecturers, and enthusiastic software business owners - decided to build a training model. Breakthrough programming, helping to improve training efficiency and quality. It is not only enough to contribute a large number of programmers to the industry, but also through it to raise the quality standards of the industry.

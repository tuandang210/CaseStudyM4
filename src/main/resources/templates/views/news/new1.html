Honda introduces special edition Honda CR-V LSE.

Hanoi, April 5, 2021 – Honda Vietnam Company officially introduces introduced special edition Honda CR-V LSE (L Special Edition) to domestic customers.

Since the launch of the new version at the end of July 2020, with the values outstanding: "Safety - Powerful - Comfortable - High-class", Honda CR-V has quickly received support of a large number of domestic customers with cumulative sales of more than 8,600 vehicles by the end of March 2021. In particular, in December 2020, CR-V is the best-selling model in the SUV segment with record sales 3,226 cars were sold, proving the increasingly strong appeal of this model.

Besides 3 versions L, G, E and 6 paint colors (Exquisite Ivory White, Silver Gray fashion, strong titanium, unique shiny black, cool blue, cool red) are being distributed company in Vietnam, with the desire to bring more personality options and great experiences to customers users, April 5, 2021, Honda Vietnam Company introduces a special version Honda CR-V LSE (L Special Edition) to domestic customers.

Honda CR-V LSE is a special version launched in the market with a single color (Unique luster black), at the same time, the details of the exterior and interior have been refined in order to increase the strong, healthy look inherent to Honda CR-V. From the outside, Honda special edition CR-V LSE impresses with dark chrome-plated borders; 18-inch wheels with 5 stylized spokes Now painted black with personality and the Black Edition logo stands out on the rear of the car.

Entering the space compartment of Honda CR-V LSE, customers will be captivated impressed by the harmony between modern design and high-quality materials on a black interior background: wood grain paneling black, the front seats are highlighted with the words Black Edition meticulously embroidered, the ceiling is colored black and the decorative borders on the dashboard, dials and doors are also turned black high fashioned.

Besides, the CR-V LSE version still possesses the core values that have been done The success of the Honda CR-V model: strong performance thanks to the 1.5L VTEC TURBO turbocharged engine with peak power up to 188hp/5600rpm; spacious and flexible space with 7-seater design; equipped with modern technology and especially Honda SENSING – Safe driving technology system Advanced technology includes 5 superior technologies:

  • Collision Mitigation Braking System (CMBS)
  • Automatic Adaptive High Beam (AHB)
  • Adaptive cruise control including low speed range (ACC with LSF)
  • Lane Departure Mitigation (RDM)
  • Lane Keeping Assist System (LKAS)

With subtle and luxurious changes in interior and exterior design and inherent values of luck performance and advanced safety technology, the special edition Honda CR-V LSE offers a complete selection of good and add personality to customers who are looking for an effective companion on all levels Street. Honda CR-V LSE will be available at Honda Automobile Distributors nationwide from May 15 4 2021 with a suggested retail price of VND 1,138,000,000.
